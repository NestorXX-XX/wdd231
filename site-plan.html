Project Site Plan

Basketball Club Meetings & Events Website

Site Name

Hoop Hub

This name was chosen because it represents a central place (“hub”) for basketball club activity—meetings, practice schedules, and events—while “hoop” clearly identifies the sport. It is short, memorable, and relevant to the project.

Optional domain idea: hoop-hub.org

Site Purpose

The site will serve as an informational hub for a basketball club focused on team meetings, practice schedules, and events. It will provide:

  • A home page with an overview of the club
  • A page showing upcoming meetings and practice times
  • A page with detailed information about games, events, and player participation
  • Dynamic display of meeting and event data using JavaScript
  • Interactive features such as viewing details in modal dialogs and saving user preferences

Scenarios

These questions reflect typical visitors (players, parents, and coordinators) and guide the content:

  • When is the next team practice and where is it held?
  • How can I see which games and events are coming up and get details or sign up?
  • Where do I find contact information for club directors or coaches?

Color Scheme

At least two colors are defined below and applied throughout this document.

  • Primary (#1a365d) — Headings, navigation background, footer background
  • Accent (#2b6cb0) — Links, buttons, and accent elements
  • Background (#edf2f7) — Page and section backgrounds
  • Text (#2d3748) — Body text and paragraphs

Typography

Two fonts are selected and used in this document:

  • Oswald — Used for main headings (h1, h2) to give a strong, sport-oriented look.
  • Open Sans — Used for body text, paragraphs, and lists for readability.
