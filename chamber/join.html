NP Membership Benefits

Non-profit organizations join at no cost. Benefits include:

  • Directory listing
  • Access to chamber publications
  • Monthly newsletter
  • Invitation to networking events
  • Community visibility

Bronze Membership Benefits

All NP benefits, plus:

  • Enhanced directory listing
  • 10% discount on chamber events
  • Quarterly training workshops
  • Social media mention opportunities
  • Business luncheon access

Silver Membership Benefits

All Bronze benefits, plus:

  • Featured listing in directory
  • 25% discount on chamber events
  • Sponsorship opportunities
  • Spotlight in monthly newsletter
  • Priority registration for workshops

Gold Membership Benefits

All Silver benefits, plus:

  • Home page spotlight placement
  • 50% discount on chamber events
  • Premium advertising options
  • Exclusive leadership roundtables
  • Annual recognition at chamber gala
